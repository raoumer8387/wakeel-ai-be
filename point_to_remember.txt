Challenge 1: Autonomous Content-to-Action Agent (Insight → Action System) Challenge Overview In today’s world, organizations are flooded with unstructured information — reports, news, dashboards, policy updates — but most AI systems stop at summarization or analysis. Real-world systems must go further. They must: ● understand information ● extract insights ● make decisions ● and take action Problem Statement Build an Agentic AI System that transforms unstructured content into actionable outcomes. Your system must: 1. Ingest unstructured input (text, reports, articles, dashboards) 2. Extract key insights 3. Analyze implications 4. Generate recommended actions 5. Simulate execution of at least one action 6. Show the resulting system state or outcome Mandatory Requirement: Google Antigravity Teams MUST use Google Antigravity as the core development platform to: ● orchestrate agent workflows ● manage reasoning and planning ● integrate tools/APIs ● handle execution of actions Use of additional LLMs is allowed, but Antigravity must be central to system logic. Example Use Cases Teams may choose any domain such as: ● Business operations ● Public policy ● Logistics / supply chain ● Financial insights ● News analysis ● Urban systems Example Scenario 1: Business Insight Input: A sales report showing declining orders in a region Output: Insight: Orders declined by 25% in Lahore Impact: Revenue loss expected Recommended Action: Launch regional discount campaign Simulated Execution: - Campaign created - Notification generated Result: Projected reach: 5,000 users Example Scenario 2: Policy / News Input: News article about fuel price increase Output: Insight: Fuel prices rising Impact: Delivery costs increasing Recommended Action: Update delivery pricing Simulated Execution: - Pricing table updated - Customer notification drafted Result: New delivery cost applied in checkout simulation System Requirements Your solution must include: 1. Content Understanding ● Process unstructured input (text, PDF, website, new article etc.) ● Extract key facts and signals 2. Insight Extraction ● Identify meaningful patterns or insights ● Avoid generic summarization 3. Impact Analysis ● Explain why the insight matters ● Connect insight to real-world consequences 4. Action Generation ● Generate clear, actionable recommendations ● Must be realistic and domain-relevant 5. Action Simulation (CRITICAL REQUIREMENT) You must simulate execution of at least one action. Simulation can include: ● Mock API call ● Dashboard update ● CRM / spreadsheet update ● Notification system ● Email/SMS generation ● Workflow trigger 6. Outcome Visualization Show: ● before vs after state ● logs of action execution ● resulting system change 7. Agentic Workflow Your system must demonstrate: ● multiple agents OR structured reasoning steps ● planning and execution flow ● traceable decision-making Deliverables Teams must submit: 1.
